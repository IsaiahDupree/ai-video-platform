## SECTION 1: THE UNIVERSAL VEO3 PROMPT FORMULA

### The Single Best-Performing Prompt Structure
To create compelling AI-generated UGC ads in Veo3, the universal prompt formula condenses successful strategies into an effective template that captures audience attention and conveys authenticity.

### Exact Template:

```
Attention-grabbing hook: [Bold claim/curiosity gap/pain point]
Main subject: [Product/Service Name]
Context: [Specific context or usage scenario]
Unique selling point: [Key benefit or differentiator]
Call to Action: ["Try now", "Learn more", "Join us"]
```

### Why Each Component Matters:
- **Attention-grabbing hook:** Establishes immediate interest and relevance by addressing a viewer's need or curiosity.
- **Main subject:** Centrally locates the product/service keeping the focus clear.
- **Context:** Grounds the ad in a relatable situation, increasing viewer connection and interest.
- **Unique selling point:** Differentiates your offering, giving audiences a reason to choose it over competitors.
- **Call to Action:** Provides clear next steps, driving immediate engagement.
